Project

The main objective of our Project is to Showcase all the different breeds of Domestic Livestock all over India and to educate people on the Diversity of all these various Breeds that are present in India.

Therefore after learning about these animals people can buy various breeds of domestic animals from different parts of India and raise these animals in their respective regions.

Planning

The main goal was to spread awareness between people about the different breeds that are present all over India and also create an Ebusiness so that people can buy Animals that have peaked their Interests.

By Making these Animals Available for sale if the owner needs to sell his animals for any financial reason we help in searching the perfect buyer so that the animal also has a good new home and the owner also is Benefitted.

Success

The service that we offer is a win-win for everybody that uses our System.

Any Browser can come to know a lot about these animals just by looking at our database.The Owner can sell his cattle for a Good price.The buyer can also look at various options before choosing the best for his Farm, and the most important thing of all is that the animal has also got a good home.
